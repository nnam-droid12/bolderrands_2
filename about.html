WHO WE ARE

Bold Errands and Delivery Drive is a fast-growing, top-notch service provider. We offer fleet management services, logistics services, vehicle tracker installation, and drivers' management. We aim to provide quality services for you at your convenience with our team of seasoned professionals. Be rest assured of our unparalleled delivery services at all times. The Fleet Management arm acts as the middle man between clients and private/commercial vehicles and their drivers. Our partnership with highly reputable taxi companies, assures clients of our credibility and value-added services when vehicles are left in our care. We hold all clients in high esteem and distance is not a barrier for us.

The Logistics arm is available for your daily door to door delivery within and outside the country. We handle client's packages with the utmost care, and are accessible, due to our always being one phone call away. We also provide tracker installation services for vehicles with tamper-proof systems and professional drivers for individual and corporate organizations. Our ultimate goal is to be a maximum dependable and reliable comfort and courier services provider. Why wait? Why delay? Please contact us today..
